Side B · About

A shop, a sofa, and the slow art of listening.

Groove Cellar opened in the basement of a Brooklyn brownstone in 1974 with one turntable, two armchairs, and a stack of Blue Note 45s. Half a century later, the armchairs are still here. So is the philosophy: music is something you sit down for.

We don't chase trends. We hunt for gear and pressings that hold up through three owners, two formats, and the test of a Tuesday afternoon.
