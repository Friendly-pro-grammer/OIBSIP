# 🎤 KK — Tribute Page

A visually rich, responsive tribute page dedicated to **Krishnakumar Kunnath (KK)** — the legendary Indian playback singer whose self-taught voice defined a generation of Bollywood and indie music.

> *"I never learned music formally. Whatever I sing comes from what I feel inside."* — KK

## 🎨 Design Highlights

- **Dark Theme** with warm gold (`#d4a853`) and soft purple accents
- **Two distinct font families**: Playfair Display (serif, headings) + Inter (sans-serif, body)
- **Multiple background colours**: deep charcoal (`#0d0d0d`), dark section (`#161619`), alt-dark (`#12111a`), gradient overlays
- **Scroll-reveal animations** using `IntersectionObserver` for progressive content loading
- **Responsive layout** that adapts cleanly from desktop to mobile
- **Micro-interactions**: hover transforms on cards, timeline items, and portrait zoom

## 🙏 About KK (1968–2022)

**Krishnakumar Kunnath** — known universally as **KK** — was an Indian playback singer and independent artist. A completely self-taught musician, he recorded over 3,500 jingles and hundreds of Bollywood songs across 11 languages. His debut album *Pal* (1999) and tracks like "Yaaron", "Tadap Tadap", "Aankhon Mein Teri", and "Khuda Jaane" remain timeless. He passed away on 31 May 2022 in Kolkata after performing his final live concert.
